Frequently Asked Questions

Can the Anker Prime 27650 charge a MacBook Pro?
Yes. With 140W output on USB-C Port 1, it charges a MacBook Pro 14" or 16" at full speed. It can charge a MacBook Air M2 to 30% in just 13 minutes.
Is the Anker Prime 27650 TSA approved for flights?
Yes. At 99.35Wh, it falls just under the international 100Wh carry-on limit. It is approved for carry-on luggage on most airlines including Australian domestic and international carriers. Always check your specific airline's policy.
How long does it take to recharge the Anker Prime 27650?
Using both USB-C input ports simultaneously (170W combined), it recharges from empty in approximately 43 minutes — one of the fastest recharge times of any power bank in this class.
What is the price in Australia?
The Anker Prime 27650mAh 250W power bank is priced at approximately $299.95 AUD as of May 2026. It is available on Amazon Australia and via Anker's Australian website.
How many times can it charge an iPhone 16?
The Anker Prime 27650 can charge an iPhone 16 Pro (3,582mAh battery) approximately 5 to 6 times from a single full charge, accounting for typical conversion efficiency.
