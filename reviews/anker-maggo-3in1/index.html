Who Should Buy the Anker MagGo 3-in-1?

The Anker MagGo 3-in-1 is the best choice if you:

  • Own an iPhone 13 or newer and want 15W Qi2 charging speed
  • Also need to charge your Apple Watch and AirPods overnight
  • Travel frequently and need a compact, foldable solution

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Anker MagGo 3-in-1 compatible with MagSafe?
Yes. It is Qi2-certified, which delivers the same 15W fast wireless charging speed as Apple's official MagSafe charger. It works with iPhone 13 and all later models.
Does it charge Apple Watch?
Yes. The dedicated Apple Watch charging pad supports all Apple Watch models including Series 9, Ultra 2, Ultra, and SE (1st and 2nd gen).
Does the Anker MagGo 3-in-1 come with a power adapter in Australia?
Yes. The Australian retail package includes a 40W USB-C power adapter with an AU-compatible plug and a 1.5m USB-C cable — no extras to buy.
What is the price in Australia?
As of May 2026, the Anker MagGo 3-in-1 Foldable Wireless Charging Station retails for approximately $149.97 AUD on Amazon Australia and via Anker's Australian store.
Is it good for travel?
Yes — it's one of the most travel-friendly 3-in-1 chargers available. It folds down to roughly the size of a deck of cards and weighs under 200g. The folding stand also works as a viewing stand for your iPhone while charging.
