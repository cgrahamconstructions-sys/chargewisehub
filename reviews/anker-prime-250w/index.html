Frequently Asked Questions

Can the Anker Prime 250W charge a MacBook Pro at full speed?
Yes. With a single device connected, USB-C Port 1 delivers up to 200W — well above the 140W maximum draw of a MacBook Pro 16". You'll get full-speed charging every time.
How many devices can it charge simultaneously?
Three devices at once — two via USB-C and one via USB-A. The total 250W is shared across ports intelligently so each device gets as much power as possible.
Does it include an Australian plug?
Yes. The Australian retail version includes a Type I (AU/NZ) plug. It also supports 100–240V input for use overseas without an adapter.
What is the price in Australia?
The Anker Prime 250W GaN wall charger retails for approximately $119.95–$129.95 AUD in Australia as of May 2026.
