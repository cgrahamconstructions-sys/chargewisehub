❓ Frequently Asked Questions
What is the best motorcycle battery charger in Australia?
The CTEK MXS 5.0 is the top choice for most Australian motorcycle owners — gentle, smart, and handles AGM and standard batteries with a proper 8-step cycle.
Can I use a car battery charger on my motorcycle?
Not recommended. Car chargers typically output too many amps for small motorcycle batteries and can damage them. Use a motorcycle-specific smart charger with a low amp setting.
How long should I charge a motorcycle battery?
A smart charger takes 4–8 hours for a typical motorcycle battery. For long-term storage, a trickle/maintenance charger runs continuously at a very low rate — indefinitely safe.
Do I need a special charger for a lithium motorcycle battery?
Yes. Lithium motorcycle batteries require a charger with a dedicated LiFePO4 mode. The NOCO Genius 5 and Optimate Lithium 4s are the best options.
