❓ Frequently Asked Questions
What is the best battery charger for a 4WD?
The REDARC BCDC1225D is the top pick — DC-DC, solar MPPT, AGM and lithium all in one unit. For home charging the NOCO Genius 10 is excellent value.
Do I need a special charger for a 4WD dual battery?
Yes. A DC-DC charger like the REDARC BCDC series charges your secondary battery from the alternator without draining the starter battery.
Can I use a regular charger on my 4WD AGM battery?
Only if it has an AGM-specific mode. Standard chargers can damage AGM batteries with incorrect charge profiles.
What amp charger do I need for a 4WD?
For a 100Ah AGM setup, a 10–25A smart charger is ideal — the NOCO Genius 10 or Projecta IC2500 are both popular Australian choices.
