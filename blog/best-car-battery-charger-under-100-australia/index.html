Best Car Battery Charger Under $100 Australia 2026

You don't need to spend a fortune to get a reliable, smart battery charger. Here are the best picks under $100.

Bottom Line Up Front: The NOCO GENIUS5 ($89 AUD) is the best battery charger under $100 in Australia. It handles 6V and 12V batteries, includes desulfation mode, and is safe enough to leave connected indefinitely. Second pick: Projecta IC800 ($75) for a great local alternative.

What to Look for Under $100

At this price point you're looking at 4–10 amp chargers — perfect for passenger cars, SUVs, motorcycles, and caravans. The key features to prioritise:

  • Smart/automatic shutoff — so you can't overcharge and damage your battery
  • Desulfation mode — recovers deeply discharged batteries
  • Spark-proof connections — safety first when clipping onto terminals
  • Multi-stage charging — extends battery life compared to basic trickle chargers
