    Wireless Charger Buying Guide 2026

    Qi vs MagSafe vs Qi2 25W: What's the Difference?

    Qi is the universal wireless charging standard (5-15W). MagSafe is Apple's magnetic system (15W for iPhone). Qi2 adds magnetic alignment to Qi (15W standard). Qi2 25W is the 2026 upgrade that doubles charging speed—look for this on premium chargers like the Belkin UltraCharge Pro.

    Key Features to Consider

    • Charging Speed: Look for 15W for fastest wireless charging
    • Multi-device: 3-in-1 pads charge phone, watch, and earbuds together
    • Build quality: Look for premium materials and good heat dissipation
    • Included adapter: Many chargers don't include a power brick

    What's New in 2026: Qi2 25W Charging

    The biggest development in wireless charging for 2026 is Qi2 25W - a new standard that doubles the speed of previous Qi2 chargers. Belkin's UltraCharge Pro and UGREEN's MagFlow are among the first to support this blazing-fast technology, charging compatible iPhones from 0-50% in just 25 minutes.
