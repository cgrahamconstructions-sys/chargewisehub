For most cars (up to 4L petrol), 400–600A peak is sufficient. The NOCO GB20 (500A) is ideal. For 4WDs and larger engines, go for 1000A+ like the NOCO GB40. For trucks and large diesels, the GB70 (2000A) is the safest choice.
