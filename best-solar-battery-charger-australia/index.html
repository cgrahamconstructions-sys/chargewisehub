❓ Frequently Asked Questions
What is the best solar battery charger for a car in Australia?
The Projecta SC110 is a popular choice for car and caravan solar charging — a 10A MPPT controller handling AGM, gel, and lithium batteries efficiently. For a complete kit, Renogy's 100W panel + MPPT bundle is hard to beat.
Do solar battery chargers work in cloudy weather?
Yes, but at reduced output. Solar panels still generate power on overcast days — typically 10–25% of rated capacity. MPPT controllers maximise output in low-light conditions.
What size solar panel do I need to charge a 12V battery?
For a 100Ah 12V battery, a 100W–200W solar panel with an MPPT controller will provide a full charge in 4–6 hours of good sunlight.
Can I use a solar charger without a controller?
Small trickle panels under 5W can be used without a controller as a maintenance charger. Anything larger requires an MPPT or PWM controller to prevent overcharging.
